My Projects

EmpowerHorizon

EmpowerHorizon is a socially-driven e-commerce platform built to empower individuals with disabilities by providing them a dedicated space to showcase and sell their handmade or customized products. The platform ensures seamless interaction between buyers, sellers, admins, and delivery personnel through clearly defined roles and secure authentication.

Key features include role-based dashboards for disabled sellers to manage product listings, track inventory, and view their orders. Buyers can browse approved products, add items to cart, place orders, and track their order status. Admins can review and approve seller products, assign orders to delivery personnel, and oversee platform analytics. Delivery personnel can view assigned orders and update their delivery status, ensuring transparency and accountability throughout the order lifecycle.

The platform incorporates robust backend validation, secure JWT-based authentication, and efficient data flow using RESTful APIs. It aims to reduce manual effort and bring independence and recognition to disabled entrepreneurs.
